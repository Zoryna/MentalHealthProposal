Adolescent Advice & Assistance

Adolescent Advice & Assistance is an organization that promotes mental wellness by providing services to help bring mental health awareness and to assist youth with mental illnesses. We want to normalize discussions on mental health and how to provide support for those who suffer from mental disorders. We are a support system for adolescents in high school.

We focus on mental illness in adolescents, or high school students, because people need a strong support system around that age. High school staff interact with students for a majority of their day in many days of the week, and it is important for staff to recognize if a student is struggling.

Our organization created a program, Mentors for Mental Health, where school staff is trained to recognize and acknowledge students who may suffer from a mental illness. Our program teaches school staff how to recognize symptoms of mental illnesses and how to properly assist them.
