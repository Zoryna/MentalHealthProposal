Our Mission

We aim to be a mental health support system for adolescents, because it is apparent that they lack resources for their mental health. We believe the best way to support adolescents is to provide services to the community they are a part of: school. Our program, Mentors for Mental Health, will train school staff to recognize and achnowledge students who may suffer from mental illnesses. It is important that students are in an environment where they know they are cared for.

Anyone can be affected by mental disorders, and we work to overall spread more awareness on mental health. Mentors for Mental Health educates school staff, but we provide educational information and advice to adolescents, and parents, on how to approach someone to discuss mental illnesses.

Our Values

People should not experience mental illnesses alone. We know that having more resources can overall increase the mental health of everyone. It is important for people to have accessible resources if they are unable afford professional help. Sometimes mental disorders can be recognized and treated sooner, and it can truly make a difference.
