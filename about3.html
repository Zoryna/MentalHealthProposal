Outcome

If Mentors for Mental Health is implemented in schools, the mental health of students will improve, and it will create a healthy, supportive environment within education. School staff will have better relationships with the students, and students will feel like they are in a community that understands and validates them. The stigma surrounding mental health will diminish, people will be more open about their mental health, and others will be more understanding and caring toward people who are struggling.
